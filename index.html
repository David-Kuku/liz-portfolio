06 / About

I've built People functions across four different companies.

Over the last six years, I've built People systems from scratch, redesigned existing ones, introduced performance management systems, led company-wide hiring, developed compensation frameworks, and partnered with leadership on organizational design and transformation.

I've learned that growing a business requires hiring the right people and providing them with the an operating rhythm that helps them do their best work. Clear goals. Accountability. Strong managers. Simple processes. Better decisions. And these are all things I enjoy building.

This site is where that experience turns into something usable for other people: playbooks, writing, and talks built from what actually happens on the inside.
